🚀 PUBLISH TO YOUR WEBSITE
Every time you add, edit or delete products in the admin, follow these steps to update your live website on GitHub Pages. It takes less than 2 minutes!
📋 COMPLETE STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE
1
Click the big orange button above to download products.json. This file contains your entire catalogue.
2
Open your browser and go to:
github.com/ankitbansal0407/Bansal-Pipes-And-Sanitaryware
3
Click on the file named products.json in the file list.
4
Click the pencil icon ✏️ (Edit this file) on the top right of the file content.
5
Select All the old text (Ctrl+A) and Delete it. Then Paste the contents of your downloaded file. Or use "Upload file" to replace it directly.
6
Scroll down and click the green "Commit changes" button. Your website will update in 30–60 seconds! ✅
💡 HOW TO ADD PRODUCT PHOTOS
  • In your GitHub repo, click Add file → Upload files
  • Create a folder called images and upload your product photos there (e.g. images/finolex-pipe.jpg)
  • In the admin, when editing a product, set the Image URL field to images/finolex-pipe.jpg
  • Download & upload products.json — the photo will appear on your website!
  • Best photo size: 600×600px, JPG or PNG format
